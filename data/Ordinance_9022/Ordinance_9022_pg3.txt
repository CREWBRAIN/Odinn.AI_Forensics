Be it ordained by the City Council of Manila, in session assembled, THAT:

SECTION 1. Gratuity Pay. - The grant of the one-time Gratuity Pay for FY 2023 not exceeding Five Thousand Pesos (PHP 5,000.00) to job order and contract of service personnel in the City Government of Manila is hereby authorized, subject to the conditions under Administrative Order No. 13, s. 2023 and applicable guidelines that may be issued by the Department of Budget and Management.

SEC. 2. Fund Source. - The Gratuity Pay in the total amount of Fifty Million Five Hundred Thirty-Five Thousand Pesos (PHP 50,535,000.00) shall be funded from the Special Activities Fund - Maintenance and Other Operating Expenses (SAF-MOOE) under Special Purpose Appropriations (SPAs) of the FY 2023 Executive Budget.

Attached are the following details:

| Annex A - Budgetary Requirement for Gratuity Pay under the SAF-MOOE of the SPAs of the FY 2023 Executive Budget. |

SEC. 3. Effectivity Clause. - This Ordinance shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

This Ordinance was finally enacted by the City Council of Manila on December 14, 2023.
