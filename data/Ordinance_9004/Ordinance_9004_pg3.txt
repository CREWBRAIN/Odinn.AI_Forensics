SEC. 2. A modified and increased amount of Sixty-Four Million Three Hundred Thirty-Two Thousand Pesos (PhP 64,332,000.00) shall be appropriated in the Annual Executive Budget CY 2024 and thereafter, under allowances for judiciary personnel assigned in the City of Manila.

SEC. 3. Repealing Clause. - All ordinances or parts thereof inconsistent with this Ordinance are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

SEC. 4. Effectivity Clause. - This Ordinance shall take effect on January 1, 2024.

This Ordinance was finally enacted by the City Council of Manila on November 14, 2023.
