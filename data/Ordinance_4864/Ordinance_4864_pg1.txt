ORDINANCE NO. 4864

4864

AN ORDINANCE APPROPRIATING THE AMOUNT OF P72,944.82 TO BE MADE AVAILABLE FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE MONEY VALUE OF THE ACCRUED VACATION AND/OR SICK LEAVES OF THE FORMER SECRETARY OF THE MUNICIPAL BOARD AND OF THE RESIGNED EMPLOYEES BELONGING TO THE COMPLEMENTS OF MEMBERS AND FORMER MEMBERS OF THE MUNICIPAL BOARD, AS PER ATTACHED ITEMIZED LIST.

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila, that:

SECTION 1. The amount of P72,944.82, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of any funds existing in the City Treasury not otherwise appropriated to be made available for the payment of the money value of the accrued vacation and/or sick leaves of the former Secretary of the Municipal Board and of the resigned employees belonging to the complements of members and former members of the Municipal Board, as per attached itemized list.

Sec. 2. This Ordinance shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila at its regular session today, March 11, 1964.

Approved by His Honor, the Mayor on March 11, 1964.
