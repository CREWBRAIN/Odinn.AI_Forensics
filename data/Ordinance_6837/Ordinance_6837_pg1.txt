ORDINANCE NO. 6884

AN ORDINANCE APPROPRIATING THE AMOUNT OF P120,000.00 TO BE MADE AVAILABLE FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A REINFORCED CONCRETE BUILDING TO HOUSE THE TAYABAS HEALTH CENTER AT TONDO.

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila, that:

SECTION 1. The amount of P120,000.00, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of the unappropriated fund existing in the City Treasury to be made available for the construction of a reinforced concrete building to house the Tayabas Health Center at Tondo.

SEC. 2. This Ordinance shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila at its regular session on November 29, 1968.

Approved by His Honor, the Mayor on December 24, 1969.
