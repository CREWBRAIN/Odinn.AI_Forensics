AN ORDINANCE PENALIZING ANY RETAILER OR ARTICLES OF PRIME NE-
CESSITY OR FOODSTUFFS, OWNER OR MANAGER OF RETAIL STORES,
RESTAURANTS AND CAFETERIAS, WHO REFUNDS TO MAKE SMALL
CHANGE TO THE EXTENT OF ONE PESO TO ANY PERSON WHO BUYS
ANY COMMODITY, GOODS, FOOD OR DRINK FROM HIS STORE OR
PLACE OF BUSINESS, AND PROVIDING FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila, thats

SECTION 1. It shall be unlawful for any retailer dealing
pmr articles of prime necessity or foodstuffs, owner or manager
of retail stores, restaurants and cafeterias to refuse to make
small change to the extent of one peso (P1.00) to any person
who buys any commodity, goods, food or drink from his store or
place of business.

SEC. 2. Any person who shall violate any of the provisions
of this ordinance shall, upon conviction thereof, be punished by
a fine of not more than one hundred pesos or by imprisonment for
not more than one month, or by both such fine and imprisonment
in the discretion of the court.

SEC. 3. This Ordinance shall take effect on September 15,
1945.

Enacted, August 24,1945
Approved, August 30,1945
