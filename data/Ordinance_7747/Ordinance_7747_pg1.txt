ORDINANCE NO. 7747

AN ORDINANCE RENAMING CONSTANCIA STREET FROM DIMASALANG STREET TO RAMON MAGSAYSAY BOULEVARD IN THE 4TH DISTRICT OF MANILA TO RUPERTO S. CRISTOBAL, SR. STREET.

Be it ordained by the City Council of Manila, that:

SECTION 1. Constancia Street from Dimasalang Street to Ramon Magsaysay Boulevard in the 4th District of Manila is hereby renamed Ruperto S. Cristobal, Sr. Street.

SEC. 2. The City Engineer is hereby directed to replace or cause the replacement of the present street signs installed in strategic places thereat.

SEC. 3. This Ordinance shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted by the City Council of Manila at its regular session today, April 30, 1991.

Approved by His Honor, the Mayor on

APPROVED:
Day 24, 1991.

GEMILIANO C. LOPEZ, JR.
Mayor
City of Manila
