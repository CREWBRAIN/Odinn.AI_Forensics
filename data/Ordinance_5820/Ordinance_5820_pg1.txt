5820

ORDINANCE NO. ______________________

AN ORDINANCE APPROPRIATING THE AMOUNT OF THREE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED SIXTY-EIGHT AND 38/100 DOLLARS TO BE MADE AVAILABLE FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE CLAIM FOR BENEFITS DUE TO THE HEIRS OF THE LATE AQUILINO DE GUZMAN, FORMER ASPHALT WORKER IN THE DEPARTMENT OF ENGINEERING AND PUBLIC WORKS, UNDER ROL-NC CASE NO. 3164 OF THE WORKER'S COMPENSATION COMMISSION.

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila, that:

SECTION 1. The amount of three thousand seven hundred sixty-eight and 38/100 dollars, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of the unappropriated fund existing in the City Treasury to be made available for the payment of the claim for benefits due to the heirs of the late Aquilino de Guzman, former asphalt worker in the Department of Engineering and Public Works, under RO4-C Case No. 3164, of the Workmen's Compensation Commission, itemized as follows:

| Item | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| 1. For compensation | 3,209.44 |
| 2. For burial reimbursement | 200.00 |
| 3. For attorney's fee | 320.94 |
| 4. For administrative fee | 38.00 |
| Total | 3,760.38 |

SEC. 2. This Ordinance shall take effect upon its approval.

Originally enacted by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila on September 14, 1966, and modified by said Body at its regular session today, October 13, 1966.

Approved by His Honor, the Mayor on October 27, 1966.

APPROVED:
Mayo
City of Manila

ATTESTED:
Serafin Manalo
Secretary to the Mayor
Heminio A. Astorga
Vice-Mayor and
Presiding Officer, Municipal Board
Secretary, Municipal Board
