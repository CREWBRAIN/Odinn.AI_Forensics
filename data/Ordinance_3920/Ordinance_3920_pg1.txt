ORDINANCE No.
3970

AN ORDINANCE APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF THREE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED PESOS TO BE MADE AVAILABLE
FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE
RETIREMENT GRATUITY OF MR. NATALIO CARVAJAL OF
THE MANILA POLICE DEPARTMENT, UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF
REPUBLIC ACT No.
1616.

Be it ordained by the Municipal Board of the City of Manila,

SECTION 1. The sum of P3,600.00, or so much thereof as may be
necessary, is hereby appropriated out of any funds (from the under-
estimated income from real estate tax for previous years accruing
during the current fiscal year 1957-1958) existing in the City Treasury not otherwise
appropriated to be made available for the payment
of the retirement gratuity of Mr. Natalio Carvajal of the Manila
Police Department, under the provisions of Republic Act No. 1616.

SEC. 2. This Ordinance shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted, November 19, 1957.

Approved,

APPROVED:
ARSENIO H. LACSON
Mayor, City of Manila

ATTESTED:
JESUS MARCOS ROCES
Vice Mayor
and Presiding Officer Municipal Board

FELIXBERTO LEWA
Secretary to the Mayor
Secretary, Municipal Board
