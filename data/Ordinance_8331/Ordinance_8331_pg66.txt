Penalty. Any person or corporation who shall violate any of the above provisions shall be punished upon conviction by final judgment by a fine of one thousand (P1,000.00) but not more than six thousand (P6,000.00) or imprisonment of one month but not more than six month or both upon the discretion of the court. The meat and carcasses, including processed meat, involved shall likewise be confiscated and disposed in accordance with the existing sanitary rules and regulations of the City of Manila.
