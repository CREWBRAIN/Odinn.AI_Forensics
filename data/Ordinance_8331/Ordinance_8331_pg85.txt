ARTICLE B

CHARGES FOR PARKING, TOWING AND TRAFFIC VIOLATIONS

SEC. 146. Parking, Towing and Traffic Violation Charges. Enumerated below are the different parking, towing and traffic violation charges:

Night Parking (sticker) for using street, sidewalk or public place or in front of their houses and places of business as a private garage or parking space:

a) For cars or jeepneys, per quarter: 247.50
b) For buses and trucks, per quarter: 330.00
c) For containerized vans/trailers, per day or fraction thereof: 20.00/day
