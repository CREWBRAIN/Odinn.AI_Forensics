SPECIAL PERMIT FEE

SEC. 132. Special Permit Fee: For purposes of this Ordinance, the City shall collect the following fees for holding events written-below:

A.) Display of fireworks, setting- up booths, motorcade, cinema billboards and any form of advertisements, per day: Php 500.00
B.) Circus of menagerie parade, carnivals, arcade or other parades using banners, floats or musical instruments: Php 1,000.00
C.) Public display/exhibits of art collections, cockpits, sports, review/license examinations, special events, fun run, conduct of group activities per day: Php 2,000.00
D.) Street dancing, requiring the closure of major street and rerouting, per day: Php 2,000.00
E.) For stage shows or floor fashion shows payable by the operator: Php 2,000.00
F.) Film shooting on location, per day: Php 5,000.00
G.) Benefit Ball/Dance, per day: Php 500.00
