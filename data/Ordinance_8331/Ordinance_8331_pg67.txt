SEC. 130. Pound and Animal Disease Control Service Fee and Charges.

A.) Animals running at large or left in public places shall be impounded by the Manila City Pound, and a corresponding fee prescribed herein for each animal so impounded shall be collected by the City through the Veterinary Inspection Board Impounding Fee

1. Large Cattle
Php 500.00/head
2. Small Animals
Php 250.00/head
3. Fowl/Poultry
Php 50.00/head

2. Subsistence Fee/day
1. Large Cattle
Php 50.00/head/day
2. Dogs, cats and other small Animals
Php 10.00/head/day
3. Fowl/Poultry
Php 5.00/head/day

3. Registration/dog license fee
1. Dog
Php 50.00
2. Surcharge
Php 10.00

B.) Disposal Fee. It shall be unlawful for any owner of dead animal to dispose its carcass anywhere. Dead animals shall be turned over to the City Pound for sanitary disposal and a fee prescribed its carcass herein shall be assessed and collected as follows:

1. Large animal
2. Small animal
3. Fowl/Poultry

C.) Adoption Fee. For unredeemed animals
1. For every dog/cat
Php 500.00/carcass
2. For every small animal
Php 100.00/carcass
3. For every fowl/poultry
Php 10.00/carcass
