SEC. 203. Repealing Clause. All ordinances, rules and regulations or part thereof
in conflict or inconsistent with the provisions of this Ordinance are hereby repealed, amended,
or modified accordingly.

SEC. 204. Effectivity. This Ordinance shall take effect immediately upon approval
and after publication

This Ordinance was finally enacted by the City Council of Manila on
November 26, 2013.
